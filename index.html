A more personal approach to residential nursing care

Happy Home is intentionally designed for a small number of residents. This allows our team to provide closer attention, better communication with families, and care plans shaped around each resident’s medical, personal, and daily living needs.

Our Services

Advanced support for complex care needs

Care is coordinated around each resident’s needs and may include support from outside physicians, hospice agencies, home health providers, and therapists.

01

Post-Surgical Recovery

Support during recovery, including assistance with mobility, medications, nutrition, and daily routines.

02

Wound Care Support

Coordinated care and close observation for residents with ongoing wound-care needs.

03

Feeding Tube Support

Daily support for residents requiring feeding tube management and nutritional assistance.

04

Diabetes Management

Medication support, observation, meal coordination, and routine communication with families and providers.

05

Hospice Support

A peaceful residential setting for residents receiving services from an outside hospice provider.

06

Long-Term & Bed-Bound Care

Respectful assistance with personal care, repositioning, hygiene, mobility, meals, and daily comfort.
